Personally, I remember things better by categories rather than by acronyms. In my mind, this equipment divides into those related to the propulsion system (fuel quantity, engine temp, engine speed, etc.), aircraft position and direction (altimeter, airspeed, compass), and safety (ELT, seatbelts, gear indicator, lights). It’s easier for me to remember 3 groups of 3-5 things each than a rote list of 13 items. It also helps connect my brain to the “why” behind the “what”.

Does this approach work for anyone else? I’m curious to hear your thoughts as an instructor about learning and memorization styles.
