by Jason Schappert | 22 comments

This week at Sun n Fun I had a chance to spend the day with some of the greatest pilots alive today. Not to many people get to hang out with the Blue Angels and lounge around in the cockpit of their giant C-130 better known as Fat Albert. In this interview I talk with Captain Ben about what it takes to be a great pilot! Enjoy!
