Diamond http://www.m0a.com/flying-instrument/comment-page-1/#comment-5712 Sun, 26 Jun 2011 20:39:00 +0000 http://www.m0a.com/?p=2428#comment-5712 I use a “Waltz Method.” 1,2,3 1,2,3. 1= attitude indicator I pause a couple of seconds on the attitude indicator. 2 and 3 is the DG and Altimeter. 2 and 3 are quicker in time than 1 the attitude indicator. I do about 4 of those and then the fifth cycle is attitude indicator to turn coordinator to VSI and back to the attitude indicator. This method has worked for me very well.
