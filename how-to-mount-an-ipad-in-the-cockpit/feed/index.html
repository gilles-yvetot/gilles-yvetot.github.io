]]> By: David Gunner Wiles http://www.m0a.com/how-to-mount-an-ipad-in-the-cockpit/comment-page-1/#comment-8644 Thu, 16 May 2013 18:47:00 +0000 http://www.m0a.com/?p=4020#comment-8644 I fly with a full size IPad and tried several different mounts. As an older pilot I prefer the bigger screen (don’t laugh young people when you hit 43 you will be reaching for glasses) but it did present a problem of where to mount. I finally discovered a goose neck mount that is mounted to my seat structure in my 172H. So when I fly the IPad sits right off my right leg between my mid-thigh and my knee. It doesn’t interfere with my vision or flight controls and because it is lower in the cockpit it doesn’t need a sunshade. It is very handy for using the scratchpad and all I have to do is glance down to reference the moving map.
