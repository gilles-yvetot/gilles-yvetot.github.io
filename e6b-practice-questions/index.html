We can always use some practice! So for this weeks video, bust out those e6b’s and let’s put them to good use. Check out the video below for full instructions! This is going to be a blast! – Jason

You're flying a course of 050 with a true airspeed of 90kts. The winds are from 070 at 15kts. What is your heading? Groundspeed? and Wind Correction Angle?




If you flew 24nm in 9 minutes how fast were you traveling (in knots)




If an aircraft burns 17 gallons in 2hrs 35mins how much fuel are you burning per hour?
