to the proper methods of flying. One suggestion that I have for you concerning this video’s subject matter is as follows.

Wouldn’t it be better if pilots used aircraft model & color in their radio calls rather then tail # at uncontrolled airports? It is easier to see a white w/red stripe Cessna 150 then it is to look for tail # 512R. If you can read the tail # you are way to close. For lesser known aircraft models, a simple description such as high (or low) wing w/color description will do. That makes it easier to match radio calls and positions of aircraft with what you see out the window, and to realize you’re dealing with a pilot who either isn’t making calls, or doesn’t have a radio.
