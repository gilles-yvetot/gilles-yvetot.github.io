Uncontrolled Airport Radio Communications

by Jason Schappert | 15 comments

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Google+

Well… With over 140 towers now officially closing due to the FAA government sequestration I figured it was time to brush up on our uncontrolled airport radio communications and operations. These airports that were formally class D and C airports will become class E and G airspace.

It’s important we as pilots understand Uncontrolled Airport operations to help increase the safety of ourselves and everyone around us.

Check out the video below:



The video was from the WAY-BACK archive deep in the MzeroA hard drives But the skill of uncontrolled airport radio communications really is timeless.
