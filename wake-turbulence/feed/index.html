Anyway.

I think another note here is to realize the wind can 'push' the wake.

So, say you're going to take off and the wind is going from right to left and you have a jet takeoff before you. Usually it's prudent to depart over the runway, but in this case it may be smarter to 'dogleg' to the right (because the wind should be blowing the wake the other direction) to further avoid the wake.

I'm sure you knew this, Jason, but just wanted to point that out as well.
