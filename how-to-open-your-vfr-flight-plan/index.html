How To Open Your VFR Flight Plan

by Jason Schappert | 13 comments

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Google+

So you’ve talked to the briefer or maybe even filed your VFR flight plan online. Now how do you open it? This can be the difficult part… How on earth do I get in touch with my local flight service station (FSS) in the air? This video uncovers all the mysteries of opening flight plans and communicating with flight service stations in the air.



What’s your experience like opening flight plans or talking with your local flight service station? There is more than one way to do things! Share your experiences below in the comment box.

  • Gary

    Always double up. I flew to Laughlin Nev. from San Diego this past Feb. and opened my flight plan with San Diego Radio on 122.2( flight plan was filed in DUATS). San Diego radio told me my flight plan was opened and requested weather reports along the way. I also opened VFR flight following with SOCAL and was passed to LA Center along the way. I was so busy with LA Center that I did not give any weather reports to San Diego/ Riverside radio. After landing in Laughlin I called to close out my flight plan,1-800-992-7433, After a long pause the tech on the phone came back and said” I’m sorry but some how your flight plan got lost after contact with you and wasn’t opened, I don’t know what to tell you!” Be Safe double up, flight plan , flight following.
