What a rush. My flight instructor wanted to see three landings and a go around before he asked me to do a full stop and drop him off on the taxiway. Then he said.. “Go do your solo”. I wasn’t expecting it and glad I had an extra shirt in my flight bag. It felt like total freedom to pilot that plane (though I talked myself through every leg of the pattern). We did the traditional cutting of the shirt tail (luckily it wasn’t my favorite) and the taking of pictures.
