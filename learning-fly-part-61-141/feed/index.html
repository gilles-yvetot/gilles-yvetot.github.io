]]> By: Martin Rottler http://www.m0a.com/learning-fly-part-61-141/comment-page-1/#comment-2065 Sat, 11 Jul 2009 06:15:11 +0000 http://www.m0a.com/?p=867#comment-2065 It might also be worth it to note the differences in flight time requirements for certification vis a vis Part 141 and Part 61. Through your CPL, the requirements are less for Part 141 certification. Once you go for a CFI, however, there is no minimum hour requirement for Part 61 while their is for Part 141.

I went though 141 at a local flight school, 141 via a university (UND) and part 61, and relished the opportunity to fly in each situation. I do think that, in spite of the lack of “book smart” development, Part 61 trains much better pilots.
