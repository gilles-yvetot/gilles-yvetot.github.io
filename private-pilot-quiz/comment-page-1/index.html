  • JackForrest

    A world Aeronautical Chart is one that is depicted in 1:1,000,000 scale. It is used to show a large portion of an Area. The WAC’s I use, for example, being in Lombok, Indonesia, show from Bali Island, through Lombok, Sumbawa Besar, all the way on to Flores.
    Also, a quick note, when planning a cross country (What WAC is most commonly used for) Be sure to use the 1:1,000,000 side (not the 1:500,000 side) of your plotter, or else you might be running into some fuel trouble!
