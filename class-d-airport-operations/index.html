Class D Airport Operations

by Jason Schappert | 14 comments

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Google+

Just talking to someone on the radios can make a student stutter and sweat. However 3 solo takeoffs and landings are required before you can be put up for your private pilot checkride. So getting familiar with control tower operations will help you immensely. Check out this video and fly along on one of my training flights!

Watch as we establish contact with the control tower, read back instructions, and even give the tower a friendly little reminder because he forgot to tell us something important!
