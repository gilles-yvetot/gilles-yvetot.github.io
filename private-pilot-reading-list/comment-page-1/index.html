    I would have to say two books that helped me gain a greater appreciation for the incredible responsibility that comes with flying are “The Killing Zone” by Paul Craig and “The Next Hour” by Richard Collins. These books underscore the fact that “A good pilot is always learning.” Together they provide guidance for continuing education and identify where pilots have made mistakes. Gaining a thorough understanding of the root causes of pilot errors will help improve GA performance and safety. I believe that pilots should have a healthy fear of flying. This helps us avoid complacency and keeps us on our toes!
