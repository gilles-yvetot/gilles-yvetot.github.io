Power Off 180

by Jason Schappert | 16 comments

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Google+

A Power Off 180 is a commercial pilot flight maneuver. However that doesn’t mean that private pilot’s shouldn’t practice it. The purpose of a power off 180 is to learn how to land your aircraft on a specific point without the use of power. This helps students realize in emergency procedures what alternate landing areas are “makable” and which aren’t. Check out this video for a great way to practice power off 180’s.
