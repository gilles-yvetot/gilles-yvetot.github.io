For me this is not a commercial maneuver, but a maneuver I teach to all my private students getting ready for their first solo. To be used if they have an engine failure on downwind. If they completely lose power, CGUMPS, power to idle, go to best glide 65 knots in my C-152) and do a horseshoe approach to their aiming point (the 1000 ft marker is my spot). Add flaps once they have their aiming point made. It is another tool in their flying toolbox.
