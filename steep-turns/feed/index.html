Having said that I have noticed another reason more often than not why a student fails to get these steep turns and just about anything else while there learning, as is the case with my student. 1) They are holding the controls with the Vulcan Death Grip and as a consequence of that. 2) They will never be able to realize  the very small pressures it takes to control the plane and they either won’t do anything or over-imput.  Crucial for them to relax and loosen up prior to them attempting any of this stuff. I always give them after we leave the terminal airspace about 5 min of them flying between 45 degrees either way no more than 15 degrees pitch up or down and no more than 5 seconds of straight and level flight initially so they can start co-coordinating and moving their feet and, after that we will get into the formal stuff.
